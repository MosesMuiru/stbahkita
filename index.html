Welcome to Stbahkita

St. Bakhita vocational training center offers hairdressing and dressmaking courses to girls and women from needy backgrounds. The program is led by qualified and experienced teachers and prepares students for a grade 3 NITA Trade Examination. Students gain real-life experience through a three-month attachment and receive practical industry-based training. The course also includes a basic computer skills program to ensure students are computer-literate.
